Double Degree in Business Analytics + Computer Science Engineering

Business Analytics: Using data analysis and statistical methods to drive informed decisions within businesses, focusing on optimizing operations, identifying trends, and making strategic choices. Roles involve data analysis, predictive modeling, and require skills in statistics, data handling, and tools like SQL, R, Python, and Tableau. Computer Science: Involves the study of computers and computational systems, encompassing software and hardware development, problem-solving using computational techniques, and areas like AI, cybersecurity, software development, and algorithms. Careers span software development, cybersecurity, AI/ML engineering, requiring strong programming skills, algorithmic understanding, and adaptability to evolving technologies.
